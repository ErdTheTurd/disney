Tips for Both Days

⚡ Lightning Lane

Set a phone alarm for 7:00am on each morning. Your first booking is the most critical — RSR on DCA day can sell out in under 5 minutes. Rebook your next LL the instant you tap into each ride.

↕ Rider Swap

Available at every height-restricted ride. One adult stays with small kids, the rest ride, then the waiting adult gets immediate access without re-queuing. Use it at: Indiana Jones (46"), Space Mountain (40"), Matterhorn (35"), Guardians (40"), Incredicoaster (48"), Grizzly Run (42").

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Group Logistics

Pick a fixed meeting spot at each park (Partners statue at DL, Buena Vista Street fountain at DCA). Consider splitting into two sub-groups at big rides to move faster. Bring your own snacks and refillable bottles — saves money and prevents hangry meltdowns between meals.
