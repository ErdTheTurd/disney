⚡ Lightning Lane Strat ⚡ — Disneyland Day

At 7:00am sharp: Book Indiana Jones first. Then chain: Space Mountain → Matterhorn → Peter Pan's Flight. Buy Rise of the Resistance as Individual LL (paid separately). Rebook every single time you tap in — don't wait until you're off the ride.

⚡ Lightning Lane Strategy ⚡ — California Adventure Day

At 7:00am sharp: Book Radiator Springs Racers first — it sells out faster than any ride at either park, sometimes within minutes. Then chain: Guardians of the Galaxy → Web-Slingers → Incredicoaster → Soarin' → Toy Story Midway Mania. Rebook after every tap-in.

Tips for Both Days

⚡ Lightning Lane

Set a phone alarm for 7:00am on each morning. RSR on DCA day can sell out in under 5 minutes. Rebook your next LL the instant you tap into each ride.

↕ Rider Swap

One adult stays with small kids, the rest ride, then the waiting adult gets immediate access without re-queuing. Heights: Indiana Jones 46", Space Mountain 40", Matterhorn 35", Guardians 40", Incredicoaster 48", Grizzly Run 42".

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Group Logistics

Pick a fixed meeting spot at each park. Consider splitting into two sub-groups at big rides to move faster. Bring your own snacks and refillable bottles — saves money and prevents hangry meltdowns.
